How To Play

Objective

  • Use the paddle to direct the ball.
  • Destroy all the bricks.
  • Win all 5 levels with the shortest play time.

Controls

Left Arrow Move Paddle Left

Right Arrow Move Paddle Right

Types of Bricks

  1. One hit to break
  2. Two hits to break
  3. Increases another brick
  4. May decrease another brick
  5. Three hits to break
